### Geometric Sequence

For consecutive elements,
$$\dfrac{a_{n+1}}{a_{n}}=r$$
The [[Sequences|sequence]]
- converges for $|r|<1$, and
- diverges for $|r|\geq1$.


#### Sum
> for $|r|<1$ :$$\sum_{n=0}^\infty ar^n =\frac{a}{1-r}$$ it converges.

> for $|r|\geq 1$:$$\sum_{n=0}^\infty ar^n =\frac{a(r^n-1)}{r-1}$$it diverges.
